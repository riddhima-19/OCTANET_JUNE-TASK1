Landing Page


A landing page is a focused, standalone web page designed to capture visitors' attention and drive a specific action, such as signing up or making a purchase. It typically includes a compelling headline, a supportive subheadline, an engaging call to action (CTA) button, and visual content like images or videos. The page highlights key benefits or features to persuade visitors of the product or service's value. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, a well-crafted landing page is visually appealing, easy to navigate, and effectively achieves its intended goal.
